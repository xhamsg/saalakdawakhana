Hakeem Ghulam Rasool Ejaz

Meet the visionary behind Saalak Dawakhana, Hakeem Ghulam Rasool Ejaz Fazal Tibb wal Jarahat, a luminary in the realm of traditional healing and herbal wellness. From dedicated his life to the study and practice of herbal remedies, particularly in addressing sexual issues in both men and women, as well as hepatitis A, B, and C. With a profound passion for preserving and propagating the age-old wisdom of natural medicine, Hakeem Ejaz is committed to providing holistic solutions for a diverse range of health issues. He started his Dawakhana in the year 2017, and since then, under his guidance, Saalak Dawakhana has become a trusted name in the world of herbal healthcare. Renowned for his expertise in blending traditional healing methods like treatment with food with contemporary wellness approaches, Hakeem Ejaz's unwavering commitment to quality and his deep understanding of herbal formulations have positioned Saalak Dawakhana as a beacon of health. The products offered by Saalak Dawakhana embody the essence of centuries-old herbal wisdom in the pursuit of optimal wellness.
